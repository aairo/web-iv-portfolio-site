All Father

This was one of my first posters made for screen printing. This year, there has been a lot of neo-nazi and white supremacist hate groups popping up, using the symbols of the Asatru religion to spread their hate. I made this poster to help the general public understand that these people are not Asatru, nor are those who happen to have runic emblims or tattoos racist.
After I posted this image on facebook, I got over 10k shares, and it has been posted to The Asatru Community’s main facebook page, various Asatru and Odinism twitter accounts, and just all over the world wide web!
Odin is the All father, not the ‘scandinavian only’ father.
